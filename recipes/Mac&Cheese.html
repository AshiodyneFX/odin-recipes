Mac & Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
  • ¼ cup butter
    • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps:

  1. Boil the Pasta
  2. Make the roux
  3. Add Cheddar and stir til melted
  4. Drain the Pasta
  5. Fold the Pasta into the cheddar sauce til coated
  6. Serve and enjoy!
